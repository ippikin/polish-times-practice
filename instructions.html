Polish Voice Installation Guide

Step-by-step instructions for downloading and enabling Polish text-to-speech (TTS) voices on your operating system

iOS (iPhone & iPad)

  • Open the system Settings app.
  • Go to the Accessibility tab.
  • Under the Vision section, tap Spoken Content.
  • Tap on Voices.
  • Scroll down and select Polish.
  • Tap the cloud icon to download your preferred voice (e.g., **Zosia**, **Paulina**, or **Siri**). We highly recommend choosing the **Enhanced** version for much better pronunciation quality.
💡 Tip: After downloading the voice, restart Safari or Chrome so the new voices appear in the drop-down list on our application.

Android (Google / Samsung)

  • Open the system Settings app on your phone.
  • Go to System or General Management.
  • Select Language & Input (or Language and Region) -> Text-to-speech output.
  • Tap the Gear icon next to your preferred engine (e.g., *Speech Services by Google*).
  • Select Install voice data.
  • Find Polish in the list and download the voice package. You can select different voice variants after downloading.
💡 Tip: Ensure **Speech Services by Google** is selected as the Preferred Engine. It provides the most natural-sounding offline and online Polish synthesis on Android.

Windows 10 / 11

  • Open the Start Menu and click Settings (or press Win + I).
  • Select Time & Language in the left sidebar.
  • Click on the Speech tab.
  • Under the Manage voices section, click the Add voices button.
  • Search for **"Polish"**, check the box, and click **Add**.
  • Windows will download and install the Polish speech engine (e.g., **Paulina** and **Adam** voices).
💡 Tip: Make sure to restart your browser (Chrome / Edge) after installation so the newly downloaded system voices are recognized by the browser.

macOS (Apple Mac)

  • Open System Settings from the Apple menu ().
  • Click on Accessibility in the left sidebar.
  • Scroll down to the Vision or Speech area and click Read & Speak.
  • Click the small **Info button ("i")** next to the System voice dropdown menu.
  • Select Polish in the left list or search for it.
  • Download your preferred voice (e.g., **Zosia**, **Ewa**, or Siri voices) by clicking the download icon.
💡 Tip: Natural Siri voices or **Enhanced** voices offer excellent pronunciation and cadence for listening practice.

After installing the voices, refresh the practice page to update the voice list in the settings panel.
